V-I relationship

current–voltage law
v = L di/dt
AC / changing I
di/dt ≠ 0
→ v ≠ 0 — voltage appears
DC steady state
di/dt = 0
→ v = 0 — short circuit
Contrast with capacitor: inductor is a short at DC; capacitor is an open at DC.

Time constant intuition — τ = L/R

Why larger R → faster response

Larger R
  • Smaller Ifinal = V/R
  • τ = L/R decreases → settles faster
Smaller R
  • Larger Ifinal = V/R
  • τ = L/R increases → settles slower
